OPTIMIZED PROMPT OUTPUT

OnPrompted returns a fully structured, model-ready prompt with clear goals, constraints, and output formatting — ready to paste into any AI.

Example output
Role: You are an email copywriter for a SaaS that helps college students manage deadlines.

Goal:
- Write a high-converting launch email announcing a new productivity app for students.
- Drive clicks to download the app within 24 hours.

Audience:
- College students (18–24) juggling classes, exams, and side commitments.
- Tech-comfortable, easily overwhelmed by long emails.

Constraints:
- 250–350 words.
- Clear subject line (3 options).
- Friendly, motivating tone, never scammy or overhyped.
- Skimmable formatting with short paragraphs and bolded phrases.

Output format:
1) Subject line options
2) Preview text
3) Full email body in markdown
4) One-line CTA to close
